FAQs

Common Questions About Domestic Extensions & Conversions

When should I bring you in on the project?
As early as possible — ideally once first-fix stage is being planned, so sockets, lighting, and circuits are positioned correctly first time.
Do you liaise directly with my builder or architect?
Yes, we're used to working from drawings and coordinating on-site directly with the rest of the build team.
Is Building Standards certification included?
Yes, we certify the electrical work to the standard your local authority requires.
How long does first fix take on a typical extension?
It depends on size and circuit count — we'll confirm a timeline once we've seen the plans.
Can you wire outbuildings and garden rooms too?
Yes, including outdoor supplies, garden offices, and outhouse conversions.
