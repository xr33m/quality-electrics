In Depth

Planning Electrical Work Around a Live Business

The biggest cost in a commercial job is rarely the materials — it's downtime. We scope every fit-out around your actual trading hours from the start, whether that means working evenings, overnight, or phasing the job area by area so the rest of the unit stays open.

LED retrofits are usually the quickest win we do for existing commercial spaces — swapping outdated fluorescent tube fittings for LED cuts running costs and reduces how often you're replacing failed tubes, and it's often done in phases without shutting anything down.

Common Concerns

What Customers Are Usually Wondering

Will this disrupt trading?

Not if we plan it properly — evenings, overnight, or phasing area by area is common, and we'll build the schedule around your hours, not the other way round.

Does the power need to be isolated during the work?

For parts of it, yes — we plan isolations for the smallest area and shortest time possible, and agree the timing with you in advance.

How long will it take?

It depends on scale — we'll give you a firm timeline once we've seen the drawings or site, not a vague estimate.
