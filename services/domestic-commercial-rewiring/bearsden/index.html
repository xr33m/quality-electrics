Home & Business Rewiring in Bearsden — Common Questions

How long does a full rewire take?
A typical two or three-bedroom tenement flat takes around a week. We'll give you an exact timeline once we've surveyed the property.
Do I need to move out during a rewire?
Not always for a partial rewire, but for a full rewire it's usually easier to have the property empty. We'll talk through the options for your specific job.
How disruptive is a rewire for detached and semi-detached homes in Bearsden?
For detached and semi-detached homes in Bearsden, we work room by room so you're never without power to the whole property, and we protect flooring and finishes throughout.
Do older properties in Bearsden need a full rewire or just a partial one?
It depends on the age and condition of the existing wiring, which we assess during an inspection rather than assuming. Many older detached and semi-detached homes in Bearsden only need a partial rewire of specific circuits — we'll be upfront about what's actually needed.
Can you rewire commercial units in Bearsden?
