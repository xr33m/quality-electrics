FAQs

EV Charger Installation in Glasgow City Centre — Common Questions

Do I need planning permission for a home charge point?
Almost never for a standard domestic installation. We'll flag it if your property is an exception, such as a listed building.
How long does installation take?
Most home installs are done within half a day, depending on cable run length and whether any consumer unit work is needed.
Can I get an EV charger fitted on a tenement and flatted properties in Glasgow City Centre?
It depends on parking. Tenement flats in Glasgow City Centre without private, off-street parking usually can't have a home charger fitted directly, since the supply and parking need to be linked to the same property. We can survey your specific situation and talk through options, including workplace or on-street charging schemes.
How much does EV charger installation cost in Glasgow City Centre?
Most home installations in Glasgow City Centre fall within a standard fixed-price range, though the exact cost depends on cable run length and whether your consumer unit needs any work first. We'll give you a clear, no-obligation quote after a quick survey — no hidden extras.
Do you cover EV charger installs across Glasgow?
Yes, we regularly install EV chargers across Glasgow City Centre and the rest of Glasgow, alongside the surrounding Glasgow areas.
