Kitchen Electrical Installations in Bearsden — Common Questions

When should I book you in relative to my kitchen fitter?
Before the units go in, ideally at first-fix stage, so sockets and circuits land in the right places once cabinets are fitted.
Can you work directly with my kitchen designer or fitter?
Yes, we regularly work from kitchen design plans and coordinate directly with the fitter.
Can you handle the electrics for a full kitchen renovation in Bearsden?
Yes — we regularly work alongside kitchen fitters in Bearsden covering everything from appliance circuits and under-cabinet lighting to sockets positioned exactly where your new layout needs them.
Do older detached and semi-detached homes in Bearsden need extra circuits for a modern kitchen?
Often yes — modern kitchens draw more power than older wiring in detached and semi-detached homes was designed for, particularly with induction hobs and built-in appliances. We'll assess your existing circuits before quoting so nothing gets missed.
How do you coordinate timing with my kitchen fitter in Bearsden?
